====


prefixes
  B ~ in/with/by
  C ~ as/like
  H ~ the
  M ~ from/out-of
  V ~ and
  ` ~ to
  L ~ for/to

  conjugations
    A ~ I
    ' ~ He
    N ~ We
    T ~ She
  
  others
    H ~ interrogative
    $ ~ that/which/whom
    V ~ past <-> future


suffixes
  ' ~ I/mine
  D ~ you/thee
  H ~ to/[possesive]
  'HM ~ theirs
  'N ~ his/hers/theirs
